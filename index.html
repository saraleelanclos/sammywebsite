ABOUT

Slutty Sammys originated as a passion project, selling sammys on a sliding scale to beloved queer community members at queer beaches in LA and NYC.
The goal of Slutty Sammys is simple - share a delicious, sensuous, sandwich with people you love and enjoy every bite.
Since our start, we have expanded into nightlife pop-ups and work events.
We are happy to work with you to fulfill your sammy dreams for your event.
Send us an email, we can't wait to talk!

For all booking inquiries please contact: sluttysammys@gmail.com
or reach us on IG @sluttysammys
